APPLE PIE

Ingredients:
  • 1 (14.1 ounce) package pastry for a 9-inch double-crust pie
  • ¾ cup white sugar, or more to taste
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 6 cups thinly sliced apples
  • 1 tablespoon butter
    • STEPS
  • 1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
  • 2. Line a 9-inch pie dish with one pastry crust; set second one aside.
  • 3. Combine 3/4 cup sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl. Add more sugar if your apples are tart.
  • 4. Layer apple slices in the prepared pie dish, sprinkling each layer with cinnamon-sugar mixture. Dot top layer with small pieces of butter. Cover with top crust.
  • 5. Bake pie on the lowest rack of the preheated oven for 10 minutes. Reduce oven temperature to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) and continue baking until golden brown and filling bubbles, 30 to 35 minutes more. Serve warm or cold.








    • CHOCOLATE CREAM

    Ingredients:
  • 3 ounces bittersweet or semisweet chocolate, chopped
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons confectioners' sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon pure vanilla extract, optional
    • STEPS
  • 1.Gather the ingredients.
  • 2. Place the chopped chocolate in a medium bowl.
  • 3. In a small, heavy saucepan, add the heavy cream and stir in the confectioners' sugar.
  • 4. Place over medium heat and bring to a boil.
  • 5.Pour the hot cream over the chocolate, stirring until smooth and the chocolate is melted.
  • 6. Cover and refrigerate for 4 hours, or until thoroughly chilled. For best results, also put the mixing bowl and beaters in the refrigerator a few hours before you plan to beat the cream.
  • 7.Transfer the chilled cream to a large (preferably chilled) bowl and add the vanilla extract. Using the chilled beaters, beat to spreading or piping consistency. Refrigerate for about 1 hour before using.
  • 8. Use on your favorite dessert and enjoy.







    • LEMON MERINGUE PIE

    Ingredients:
    Pie crust :
  • 1 1/4 cups all purpose flour 156g
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup cold butter cut into cubes
  • 3-4 tablespoons cold water
    • Filling :
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar 300g
  • 1/2 cup corn starch 60g
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • zest from 2 lemons
  • 2 tablespoons butter
    • Meringue :
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon corn starch 8g
  • 4 egg whites
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • pinch of salt
  • 1/2 cup sugar 100g
    • STEPS PIE CRUST
  • 1. In a medium bowl, stir together the flour, sugar and salt.
  • 2. Cut in butter with a pastry cutter or fork until you see pea-sized chunks of butter remaining.
  • 3. Gradually add in cold water, stirring each time until you can press it together.
  • 4. Form it into a disk, wrap in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or freeze for 30 minutes until chilled.
  • 5. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
  • 6. Roll out to fit a 9″ pie plate.
  • 7. Press into the pie plate and over the sides. Trim the edges and crimp or leave as desired. Poke the bottom and the sides with a fork all over to help prevent puffing.
  • 8. Bake for 10-12 minutes until light golden brown. Set aside while you make the filling.
    • STEPS FILLING
  • 1. In a medium saucepan, whisk together the sugar, water, corn starch, egg yolks, lemon juice and lemon zest.
  • 2. Cook over medium  medium-high high, whisking constantly, until thickened  it will be thick, slightly thicker than pudding when warm. This can take 10-15 minutes, but dont rush it. Remove from heat and stir in butter.
  • 3. Pour hot filling into the pie crust and make the meringue.
    • STEPS FILLING
  • 1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  • 2. In a small saucepan, whisk together the water and corn starch. Cook over medium heat until thickened and clear. Set aside but keep warm.
  • 3. In a clean bowl with clean beaters, beat egg whites, vanilla and salt until soft peaks form.
  • 4.Gradually add in the sugar, beating until stiff glossy peaks form.
  • 5. Gradually add in the warm corn starch mixture, beating constantly, until completely incorporated and meringue is light and fluffy and holds stiff peaks.
  • 6.Spread meringue onto hot pie filling (its important that the filling is still hot!) and spread right to the edge of the pie crust. The meringue must touch the edges all the way around
  • 7. Bake at 375 F just until the tops of the meringue are light golden brown  dont overbake.
  • 8. Let rest on the counter on a wire rack for 1-2 hours, then refrigerate for 5-6 hours until chilled, or overnight.
  • 9. Slice and serve.
